Explore Manila's Streets

Roxas Boulevard

Roxas Boulevard is a major road in Manila known for its scenic views of Manila Bay, perfect for watching the beautiful sunset.

Quiapo

Quiapo is a lively and historic street, famous for the Quiapo Church and its bustling markets.

Binondo

Binondo, Manila's Chinatown, is a vibrant area filled with shops, restaurants, and a rich cultural heritage.

Mendiola

Mendiola is known for its proximity to the Malacañang Palace and has historical significance in Filipino politics.
